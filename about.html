About Weather Wear

Know what to wear in any weather!

Weather Wear is a web app that makes reccomendations on what to wear based on the weather.

Using the Weather Underground API, this app allows you to be spared the hassle of going outside in a weather inappropriate outfit.

Created by the ingenious Team Iguanas(Jasmine, Lulu, and Diana) with help from our mentor Caroline for the WSHackathon
